MtCars

EDA process using python

While exploring the mtcars dataset, we found that cars with fewer cylinders, especially 4-cylinder ones, tend to be more fuel-efficient with higher miles per gallon (MPG). On the other hand, cars with higher horsepower and weight usually have lower mileage, showing that more powerful, heavier cars consume more fuel. There’s also a strong negative correlation between MPG and weight, meaning the heavier the car, the less fuel-efficient it is. These insights help us understand the trade-offs between performance and fuel economy in cars.
